## 3. Literature matrix — prior work vs the four integration gaps

The contribution is integration and attribution, not invention of any single
technique. ● = substantially addressed; ◐ = partially; ○ = not addressed.

| Prior work | G1: continuous, carbon-priced cost–resilience **threshold** | G2: **attribution** of the threshold to design parameters | G3: **technology isolation** (matched backbone) | G4: **dynamic** (frequency) feasibility of the same portfolios |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| PH island HRES sizing — Ocon/Castro/Pascasio \cite{castro2022techno,ocon2019energy,pascasio2021comparative} | ◐ LCOE vs config | ○ | ○ | ○ |
| Resilient PH electrification — Bertheau & Blechinger \cite{bertheau2018resilient} | ◐ resilience-aware cost | ○ | ○ | ○ |
| NREL survivability — Marqusee et al. \cite{marqusee2021resilience}; metric \cite{mishra2023microgrid} | ◐ cost + survivability, fuel-limited outage | ○ | ○ | ○ |
| Battery–hydrogen techno-economics — Alharbi et al. \cite{alharbi2025comparative} | ◐ config cost comparison | ○ | ○ | ○ |
| Grid-forming / frequency — Liu, Shahgholian, Chamorro, Olasoji \cite{liu2016comparison,shahgholian2025droop,chamorro2020innovative,olasoji2024review} | ○ | ○ | ○ | ● dynamics only, no techno-economics |
